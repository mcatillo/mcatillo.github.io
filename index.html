About me:

I am currently working at ETH of Zurich as postdoctoral research in theoretical physics. My work focuses mainly on theoretical physics, in particular Lattice QCD, in which I am working with Prof. Marina Marinkovic.

Research interests:


Theoretical particle physics
Non-perturbative methods in QCD
Random Matrix Theory
Theoretical aspects of lattice gauge theories
Algorithms for lattice QCD
Quantum computation for lattice gauge theories (just recently)
